Zeus

A drop-in turnkey datawarehouse solution for IPTV flexible enough to be customised to your environment

Building datawarehouses with the data stored in IPTV platforms can be complex, time-consuming, and fraught with error for those that do not know, or yet to have experience of such projects.

Zeus represents the result of work for major European Telco clients for international presence and their need for highly targeted and focused advertising campaigns. Designed from the start to access your native internal OSS / BSS APIs and interfaces, this product integrates easily, and naturally to your existing IPTV platform.

This leaves you free to break out all that data, for the "single-truth" that your organisation needs to effectively market and sell to the ever-growing legions of IPTV market. Unlock your hidden revenue streams now.

 

Socrates

A Geographic Plugin for your Zeus platform

What about adding Geographic information to your Zeus installation? You have a huge data warehouse of IPTV customer data, and you have determined the "single-truth", and have had a reasonable amount of success marketing to these customers given their historical data. Socrates enables you to unlock even more treasure from these profiles, and customer-scards you have created.

 

Plato

Direct Sales Marketing and Campaign Manager plugin for your Zeus platform

Direct marketing module that plugs directly into the platform and enables, mailshots, e-mail campaigns, telesales and telesupport, and fax distributions.

Plato has the advantage that it contains 50 already defined Advertising Campaigns already canned into the product. As these campaigns are defined my 'Plato Policies' you can mix-and-max, extend, delete, and create your own, in complete freedom. Make Plato your direct sales Campaign Manger, and boost your sales sky-high!

 

Aristotle

Customer Profiling Plugin for your Zeus Platform

Leverage the full power of your database. Quickly build accurate profiles of your customers, and target your sales activities as effectively as possible.

Aristotle not only knows how to drill down into your historical data, it can also access heterogeneous sources such as VoD content, Live-TV services, subscriptions to RDP, RPC and SOAP based Web-Services, connection times, EPG HD Recording schedules, favourite Live-TV channels, Remote Control activity, and many more. And thanks to its plug-in architecture you can easily add custom functions to meet your organization's specific needs, or work with Questor to develop additional providers.
